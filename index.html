About Me

I live at the intersection of technology and creativity. I teach business technology, web development, and problem-solving to college students and professionals, helping them navigate and make sense of a complex digital world. At the same time, I’m deeply connected to my artistic side—as a musician, vocalist, composer, synthesist, percussionist, producer, and visual designer. I love exploring new ways to make ideas real and humans thrive.

My love of science fiction fuels much of my imagination. I’m especially drawn to positive, egalitarian, democratic visions of the future such as the power and freedom of Iain M. Banks’ Culture, the ideals of Star Trek, and the grounded compassion of Becky Chambers. These stories, alongside the practical spirituality of Alan Watts and Eckhart Tolle, shape the way I think about possibility, presence, and purpose.
